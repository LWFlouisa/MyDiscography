### SONNET DE RENGA, PLUIE DE NOIR [ OS PEHUMOS ENEROS ]

Its raining
in Georgia.
Its raining
with hard blowing wind gusting.
The midnight shower rains hard.

Hammering, pattering, whispering.
At morning it fades.
Hammering, pattering, whispering.

Its raining
in Georgia.
Its raining
with hard blowing wind gusting.
The midnight shower rains hard.

Tonight.
