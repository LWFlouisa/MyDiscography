## ODE TO MOONLIGHT
Under moonlight<br />
where mother Luna shines<br />
Come shadows<br />
brethren of moonlit midnight<br />
where the statues lurk over<br />
the corpses under the desert.<br />
Under moonlight.

The snow now falling<br />
gives me a short chilling wind<br />
with crows on signs.
